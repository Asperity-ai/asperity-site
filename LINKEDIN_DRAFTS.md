### Notes on the post

- **Length:** ~210 words. LinkedIn's sweet spot is 150–250 for engagement. This fits.
- **Hashtags:** restrained, 5. More than that reads as desperate. Skip the generic #AI / #Innovation buzzword tags.
- **Tagging:** don't tag advisors or VCs as engagement-bait. If a specific person directly enabled the launch (e.g., a TM attorney, a local manufacturing intermediary), thank them in a comment after the post is up — not in the body.
- **First 200 chars** are critical — they show in the preview before "see more." The opening line + first sentence of paragraph 2 are what your network reads before deciding to expand. Make sure that block is strong on its own.
- **Comments strategy:** for the first 30 min after posting, reply to every comment within ~2 min. LinkedIn's algorithm boosts posts with early engagement.
